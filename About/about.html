    About Us

    Who We Are

    YEG Givers Support Centre Ltd is a community-driven, not-for-profit organization based in Edmonton, Alberta. We provide essential services for individuals and families experiencing hardship—including newcomers, low-income households, and those facing crisis.

    Our Story

    YEG Givers was born from years of grassroots community work led by our Founder, Fadipe.O, and Co-Founder, Toyosi.O. What began as quiet, underground support for vulnerable individuals has grown into a formal support centre committed to equity, dignity, and hope.

    Our Vision

    A thriving, empowered community where everyone has access to the support, resources, and opportunities they need to live with dignity and purpose.

    Our Mission

    To provide compassionate, inclusive, and accessible support services that uplift individuals and families through community engagement, mental wellness, and basic needs support.

    About Fadipe.O – CEO & Founder.

    Fadipe.O is a compassionate leader, community advocate, and the Founder and CEO of YEG Givers Support Centre Ltd, established in 2025. Before launching the organization, Fadipe worked underground for several years, offering informal support to individuals and families facing hardship. These behind-the-scenes efforts—rooted in empathy and a deep understanding of community needs—eventually gave rise to YEG Givers, a formal platform designed to provide essential resources, emotional support, and empowerment. Fadipe holds a solid educational background in social sciences and human services, complemented by extensive professional experience in community outreach, non-profit work, and frontline support roles. This unique combination of academic knowledge and real-world service has equipped Fadipe with the tools to lead with both heart and strategy. With a passion for equity and inclusion, Fadipe continues to champion accessible support systems and inspire a culture of giving throughout Edmonton and beyond. Through YEG Givers, Fadipe empowers others to become change-makers in their communities, one act of kindness at a time.

    About Toyosi, O – Co-Founder

    Toyosi, O is the Co-Founder of YEG Givers Support Centre Ltd and a passionate advocate for peace, equity, and community well-being. With a Master’s degree in Peace and Conflict Resolution, Toyosi brings a deep understanding of human relationships, social justice, and the power of community-based solutions to every aspect of her work. In addition to her role at YEG Givers, Toyosi currently serves as an Educational Assistant, where she supports diverse learners and helps create inclusive, nurturing environments for students to thrive. Her hands-on experience in education complements her academic background, allowing her to connect with individuals of all ages and backgrounds in meaningful ways. Toyosi’s calm presence, conflict-resolution skills, and dedication to community healing have been instrumental in shaping the vision and programs of YEG Givers. She believes in the strength of unity, the importance of compassion, and the power of giving back.

    YEG History

    YEG Givers Support Centre Ltd. is a non-profit organization based in Edmonton, Alberta, dedicated to assisting low-income and newcomer families by providing food donations, household items, counseling services, and educational programs. Founded by Yemi Fadipe (RSW) and Toyosi Ogundare, our mission is to foster a supportive and self-sufficient community for newcomers, ensuring they have the resources necessary to thrive in their new environment. Positioned as a vital support system for Edmonton’s immigrant and low-income communities, we are committed to bridging gaps in community resources, fostering resilience, and empowering families through our holistic approach. Our core values—compassion, inclusivity, empowerment, integrity, and community-driven service—guide our efforts in delivering impactful programs tailored to the unique needs of those we serve.

    YEG Givers Support Centre Ltd
