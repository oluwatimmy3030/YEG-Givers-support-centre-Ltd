Welcome to YEG Givers Support Centre Ltd

At YEG Givers, we believe in the power of compassion, community, and collective giving. Founded in 2025, our mission is to support individuals and families facing life’s toughest challenges—whether it’s meeting basic needs, accessing mental health support, or adjusting to life in a new country. We are here to uplift, empower, and walk alongside you

Join the Movement. Be a Giver.
